CANTILEVER SLIDE GATE SPECIFICATIONS
========================================

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Gate shall be cantilever slide type
- All steel shall be hot-dip galvanized
- Gate shall operate smoothly with minimal force
- Design shall comply with local building codes

MATERIALS:
- Steel Grade: A572_50
- Infill Type: chain_link
- Hardware: Stainless steel where exposed
- Finish: Hot-dip galvanized per ASTM A123

PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS:
- Wind Load: 33.5 m/s
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +60°C
- Service Life: 25 years minimum
- Maintenance: Annual inspection required
